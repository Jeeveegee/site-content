**Region Code**: AMAS
**Region Director**: Federico Navarro

VATSIM Americas Region is split into seven divisions:

* [Brazil Division (VATBRZ)](https://vatsim.com.br/)
* [Canada Division (VATCAN)](https://vatcan.ca/)
* [Caribbean Division (VATCAR)](https://vatcar.net/)
* [Central America Division (VATCA)](https://www.vatca.net/), comprised of Guatemala, Belize, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Costa Rica & Panama.
* Mexico Division (VATMEX)
* [South America Division (VATSUR)](https://www.vatsur.org/app/#about) includes Argentina, Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Peru, Ecuador, Uruguay, Venezuela & Paraguay.
* [United States of America Division (VATUSA)](https://www.vatusa.net/)

The region is home to hundreds of controllers and even more pilots as well as Virtual Airlines. Our objective is to provide professional Air Traffic Control while ensuring a fun and friendly environment for all in our airspace.
